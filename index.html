Welcome to the OSC official chess club website.

Here you can find information about our club, including meeting times, contact information, and more. We hope you enjoy your visit!

The first thing to know about the OSC Chess Club is that we are a group of chess enthusiasts who meet every week to play chess. We welcome players of all skill levels, from beginners to grandmasters. We have a friendly and welcoming atmosphere, and we are always happy to help new players learn the game. If you are interested in joining us, please come to one of our meetings. We look forward to meeting you!

About Us

The OSC Chess Club was founded in 2010 by a group of students who shared a passion for chess. Since then, the club has grown to include players of all ages and skill levels. Our mission is to promote the game of chess and provide a welcoming and inclusive environment for players to enjoy the game. We believe that chess is a great way to develop critical thinking skills, improve concentration, and have fun. We are always looking for new members to join us, so if you are interested in chess, we would love to have you as part of our club.

Meeting Times

Our meetings are held every Tuesday and Thursday at lunchtime in the Pukeko Pod. We play casual games, discuss chess strategy, and have fun. We hope to see you there!
